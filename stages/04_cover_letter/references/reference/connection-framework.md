# Connection Review Framework

Review the letter in this order. Stop at the first tier that fails badly enough to make later tiers moot.

1. **Truth** — every claim is supported by the verified record. A letter that overstates cannot be fixed
   by better targeting.
2. **Role fit** — the letter addresses the actual responsibilities of the target role, not a generic
   category of work.
3. **Employer specificity** — at least one sentence identifies a need, operating environment, or
   responsibility that belongs to this employer, connected to verified experience. The test: could this
   paragraph be mailed to a competitor unchanged? If yes, it fails.
4. **Evidence** — the strongest experience claim carries a result or decision outcome, not just scale.
   "Coordinated 12 aircraft" is scope; what improved, held, or became possible is evidence.
5. **Tone and length** — calm, exacting, respectful; no borrowed enthusiasm, no military prestige framing,
   no filler. A civilian reader should finish it in under a minute.
