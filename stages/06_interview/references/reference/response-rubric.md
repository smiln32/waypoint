# Response Rubric

Score 0–4 using observable evidence only:

- Relevance: answers the question and competency.
- Ownership: distinguishes the candidate's decisions.
- Context: gives only the setting needed.
- Evidence: includes concrete action and defensible result.
- Translation: uses civilian-accessible language.
- Reflection: shows learning or calibration.
- Delivery: clear structure, appropriate length, understandable language.

Scores locate a practice need; they do not predict hiring. Support every score with evidence. Missing facts receive lower scores, never invented completions.
