# Examples

Question: “Tell me about a time you disagreed with a supervisor.”

Passage: “I knew the maintenance chief was wrong, but in the Marines you follow the chain of command. We handled it.”

**High — Judgment remains invisible**

What the employer is testing: Whether you can challenge a decision constructively while preserving working relationships and safety.

Why it fails: “We handled it” removes your action and outcome. “The chief was wrong” gives a conclusion without showing your evidence or how you raised the concern.

Candidate's task: Identify what you observed, how you communicated the concern, what decision followed, and what you learned. Do not change the outcome.
