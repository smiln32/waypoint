# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Recently separated enlisted U.S. Marines and other veterans translating verified military experience into civilian technical and operational careers.

## Product Purpose

Turn verified experience into a transparent career-search workspace: a civilian resume, editor-guided revisions, job matching, application tracking, company research, and repeatable interview practice.

## Positioning

Translate the service record without erasing the person who earned it.

## Brand Personality

Calm, exacting, and respectful.

## Anti-references

Avoid camouflage, flags as decoration, stencil type, rank insignia, tactical-game aesthetics, generic AI dashboards, motivational slogans, and opaque decisions.

## Design Principles

- Show the reasoning behind every recommendation.
- Preserve user agency at every revision point.
- Present one manageable next action before secondary detail.
- Treat military experience as evidence, not mythology.
- Make accessibility and plain language operational requirements.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA with strong contrast, keyboard operation, visible focus, reduced-motion support, 44-pixel touch targets, plain-language labels, and no reliance on color alone.
